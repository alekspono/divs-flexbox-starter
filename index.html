Regarding Opera: Classical music has always been a part of my life – having been raised as a non-professional but active violinist. But as a child the one type of classical music I could not, would not like, was opera. Then, as often happens, in college I took an elective about the music of Mozart, and once I saw a performance of “The Marriage of Figaro”, I was hooked.

I love looking at art. It is akin to therapy for me. One of my favorite artists is Wassily Kandinski. Though I do not claim to understand the true depth of his work, it brings me so much peace to look at the colors, lines and motion in his work.

Learning languages is a hobby/necessity for me. It stems from being an immigrant, and having had to immerse myself into a completely new language culture as a child. I now often crave this, and have been able to realize it primarily through travel and the chance to practice both in and outside of the classroom.
